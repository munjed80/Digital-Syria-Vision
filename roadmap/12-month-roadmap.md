## Executive Summary

A 12-month roadmap covering the first year after the programme decision, in
four quarterly waves. This is a **planning proposal**; numbers are scenario
estimates and depend on funding, sanctions, and absorptive capacity (see
`docs/canonical-metrics-and-assumptions.md`).

---

## Q1 (Months 1–3) — Foundation

See `roadmap/first-100-days.md`. Key outcomes:

- NDTC and ministry CDOs in place.
- Reference architecture endorsed.
- First five policies in circulation.
- Pilot in one governorate with two priority services.
- SY-CERT 24/7 watch operational.

---

## Q2 (Months 4–6) — Pilot expansion

| Track | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Pilot | Two pilot governorates; 5 priority services live for invited citizens. |
| NDID | First 5 enrolment centres operational; first 50,000 enrolments completed. |
| GovCloud | SyriaGovCloud primary site (Damascus) accepting first non-critical workloads. |
| API Gateway | First three ministry APIs onboarded behind the Gateway. |
| Payments | GPG sandbox open for ministry integration testing; no live citizen payments yet. |
| Cybersecurity | First independent penetration test of NDID and GovCloud completed; findings published in summary. |
| Citizen rights | Citizen Rights Charter ratified by NDTC; complaints channel live. |

---

## Q3 (Months 7–9) — Public soft launch

| Track | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Citizens | First public soft launch in pilot governorates; invited cohort of citizens (target: 100,000). |
| NDID | Cumulative enrolment scenario: 250,000–500,000 citizens. |
| Services | 10 priority services live in pilot governorates. |
| Payments | GPG live for low-value, low-risk services in pilot governorates. |
| Cybersecurity | SY-CERT first national exercise completed (tabletop + technical). |
| Reporting | Quarterly programme report; first independent audit by an external party. |

---

## Q4 (Months 10–12) — National rollout decision

| Track | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Decision | Council of Ministers takes a national-rollout decision based on pilot evidence. |
| Citizens | Pilot extended to a third governorate. |
| Services | 15 priority services live in pilot governorates. |
| NDID | Cumulative enrolment scenario: 1–1.5 million citizens. |
| Disaster recovery | First DR drill completed against the secondary site (`docs/disaster-recovery-and-backup.md`). |
| Open data | First open-data datasets published on a `data.gov.sy`-style portal. |
| Reporting | Annual programme report. |

---

## End-of-Year-1 cumulative scenario

- 3 governorates piloting.
- 15 priority services live in those governorates.
- 1 — 1.5 million NDID enrolments (scenario).
- SY-CERT 24/7 with 12+ analysts.
- One independent audit and one tabletop exercise on record.
- Five policies in active use; Citizen Rights Charter ratified.

These are scenario estimates and must be re-baselined every quarter.

---

## What is explicitly NOT promised in Year 1

- No nation-wide simultaneous launch.
- No claim of "Top 70 UN ranking" within Year 1 (that is a 5-year target).
- No claim of universal NDID coverage.
- No claim of 100% paperless services.
