## Executive Summary

This roadmap defines the first 100 days of the Digital Syria Vision
programme, from the day a programme decision is made by the Council of
Ministers (Day 0). It complements `docs/100-day-action-plan.md` by adding a
clearer governance, procurement, and risk-management track. It is a planning
proposal.

---

## Day 0 — Programme decision

- Council of Ministers approves the Digital Syria Vision charter.
- Ministry of Communications named lead ministry for execution.
- An interim NDTC chair is appointed.

---

## Days 1 — 30: Foundation

| Track | Milestone | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Governance | Interim NDTC convened (one official per relevant ministry). | PMO |
| Governance | Programme charter, decision rights, escalation path published. | NDTC |
| Governance | Each ministry nominates a Chief Digital Officer (CDO). | Ministers |
| Legal | Data Protection Authority (DPA) function established under existing law (interim). | MoJ |
| Legal | Digital Identity Bill drafting kicked off. | MoJ + NDTC |
| Architecture | Reference architecture endorsed (NDID, GovCloud, Gateway, GPG, SY-CERT). | NDTC |
| Cybersecurity | SY-CERT interim team appointed (3–5 senior staff). | NDTC |
| Communications | Public website published with the proposal text and FAQ. | NDTC |

## Days 61 — 100: Pilot and demonstrable progress

| Track | Milestone | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Pilot | Pilot governorate selected (recommended: Damascus + one secondary, e.g. Latakia or Tartus, based on connectivity readiness). | NDTC |
| Pilot | Two of the three priority services running end-to-end in pilot mode for invited citizens. | Lead ministries |
| Pilot | Citizen feedback channel live (web + phone). | NDTC |
| NDID | Enrolment-centre design finalised; first two enrolment centres physically prepared. | Ministry of Interior |
| Cybersecurity | SY-CERT 24/7 watch-floor operational at minimum staffing (4-shift rotation). | SY-CERT |
| Reporting | First quarterly programme report published, with honest red/amber/green status. | NDTC |
| Risk | Risk register reviewed and updated quarterly thereafter. | NDTC |

---

## Day 100 deliverables (cumulative)

- A working NDTC and ministry CDO network.
- Five endorsed policy documents.
- A published reference architecture.
- A published interoperability standard.
- A pilot in at least one governorate, with at least two services live.
- A SY-CERT 24/7 watch capability.
- A first transparent quarterly report.

---

## Realism notes

- Sanctions, currency volatility, and procurement constraints will likely push
  some milestones by 1–2 months. The roadmap should be re-baselined at Day 60
  with honest variance.
- A "soft launch" in one governorate is preferable to a national launch.
- No claim should be made about "users on NDID" until enrolment volumes are
  measurable.
