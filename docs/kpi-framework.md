## 1. Measurement Framework

The Digital Syria Vision KPI framework measures performance across four dimensions:
1. **Program Delivery** — Are we building what we committed to?
2. **Service Quality** — Are the services good?
3. **Citizen Outcomes** — Are citizens benefiting?
4. **System Performance** — Are the systems reliable and secure?

## 4. Ministry-Level KPIs

Each ministry tracks and reports:

| KPI | Frequency |
|-----|----------|
| Services digitized (count and %) | Monthly |
| Online service transactions | Monthly |
| Ministry portal uptime | Monthly |
| Citizen complaints about e-services | Monthly |
| Staff trained in digital skills | Quarterly |
| Ministry digital maturity score | Annual |

### Ministry Digital Maturity Assessment

Assessed annually by DGA across 5 dimensions:

| Dimension | Score 1 | Score 3 | Score 5 |
|-----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Leadership | No CDO | CDO appointed | CDO at Deputy Minister level |
| Strategy | No digital strategy | Strategy exists | Strategy implemented and monitored |
| Services | No online services | Some services online | All services online |
| Data | No data management | Data catalogue exists | Data shared via APIs |
| Security | No security plan | Basic security | Full GCBS compliance |
