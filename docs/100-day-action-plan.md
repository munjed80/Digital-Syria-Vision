# 100-Day Action Plan
**Digital Syria Vision — Immediate Launch Program**

|---|---|

## Overview

The first 100 days establish the organizational, legal, and technical foundation for the Digital Syria Vision program. This plan is designed to demonstrate visible progress to citizens and build institutional momentum.

**Core Principle:** Move fast on foundations, visible quick wins, and legal enablement simultaneously.

---

## Day 1–10: Mobilization

### Organizational
- [ ] Appoint Digital Government Authority Director General
- [ ] Constitute National Digital Transformation Council (NDTC)
- [ ] Identify 30 digital transformation champions across all ministries
- [ ] Establish Program Management Office (PMO)
- [ ] Sign memoranda of understanding with international technical partners

### Legal
- [ ] Draft Digital Government Framework Law for Cabinet review
- [ ] Identify regulatory barriers to digital service delivery
- [ ] Brief Parliament on Digital Syria Vision program

### Technical
- [ ] Conduct baseline security assessment of all ministry networks
- [ ] Commission national connectivity assessment
- [ ] Complete detailed infrastructure requirements analysis
- [ ] Issue procurement notices for GovCloud Phase 1

---

## Day 11–30: Legal and Procurement

### Legal
- [ ] Cabinet approves Digital Government Framework Law
- [ ] Parliament fast-tracks legislative review
- [ ] Draft Data Protection and Privacy Law (for consultation)
- [ ] Draft Digital Identity and e-Signature Law
- [ ] Issue mandatory API-first development circular to all ministries

### Procurement
- [ ] Evaluate GovCloud infrastructure bids
- [ ] Issue procurement for National API Gateway platform
- [ ] Begin procurement for SY-CERT tooling and training
- [ ] Procure citizen portal platform (open source preferred)
- [ ] Contract three international digital government advisors

### Partnerships
- [ ] Sign MOU with ITU for cybersecurity capacity building
- [ ] Sign partnership with UNDP for governance support
- [ ] Engage Estonian e-Governance Academy for knowledge transfer
- [ ] Initiate contact with World Bank for Phase 1 financing

---

## Day 31–60: Infrastructure and Platform Launch

### GovCloud Phase 1
- [ ] Award GovCloud construction/procurement contract
- [ ] Begin data center fit-out (Damascus primary site)
- [ ] Deploy temporary government cloud on interim infrastructure
- [ ] Migrate first 3 ministry websites to GovCloud

### Citizen Portal (Beta Launch)
- [ ] Launch portal.gov.sy in beta
- [ ] Activate 20 priority government services online
  - National ID renewal
  - Birth certificate requests
  - Business registration inquiry
  - Tax payment portal
  - University enrollment status
  - Property registration inquiry
  - Driver's license renewal
  - Passport status tracking
  - Social welfare registration
  - Health card application
  - Building permit inquiry
  - Vehicle registration
  - Marriage/divorce certificate
  - Educational certificate authentication
  - Import/export license inquiry
  - Employment contract registration
  - Pension registration
  - Electricity connection application
  - Water connection application
  - Telecommunications service request
- [ ] Deploy multilingual support (Arabic primary, English secondary)
- [ ] Launch citizen feedback mechanism

### National API Gateway
- [ ] Deploy API Gateway platform
- [ ] Connect first 5 ministries
- [ ] Publish API standards documentation
- [ ] Launch developer portal

### SY-CERT Foundation
- [ ] Appoint SY-CERT Director
- [ ] Hire initial team of 15 security analysts
- [ ] Deploy Security Operations Center tooling
- [ ] Establish 24/7 monitoring capability (basic)
- [ ] Issue first government security alert
- [ ] Join OIC-CERT network

---

## Day 61–90: Digital Identity Pilot

### NDID Pilot Program — Damascus
- [ ] Open 10 NDID enrollment centers in Damascus
- [ ] Train 100 enrollment officers
- [ ] Enroll first 50,000 citizens in National Digital ID
- [ ] Issue 50,000 digital credentials (app + card)
- [ ] Pilot digital login for 5 government services
- [ ] Evaluate and refine enrollment process

### Payment Gateway Proof of Concept
- [ ] Deploy payment gateway sandbox
- [ ] Integrate 3 payment methods (bank transfer, card, mobile money)
- [ ] Process test transactions for 5 services
- [ ] Security audit of payment infrastructure

### Government Skills Program
- [ ] Launch Digital Government Academy (online)
- [ ] Enroll 500 civil servants in digital skills program
- [ ] Train 30 ministry IT officers on new platform

---

## Day 91–100: Assessment and Scale Planning

### Assessment
- [ ] Publish 100-Day Progress Report
- [ ] Citizen satisfaction survey on portal (target: 50,000 responses)

## Risk Register for 100-Day Plan

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Procurement delays | High | High | Pre-bid engagement; streamlined evaluation |
| Key personnel not identified | Medium | High | International recruitment; diaspora outreach |
| Infrastructure delivery delays | Medium | High | Interim cloud on existing infrastructure |
| Low citizen uptake of portal | Medium | Medium | Aggressive awareness campaign; incentives |
| Cybersecurity incident at launch | Low | Very High | Pre-launch penetration testing; IR plan |
| Parliamentary delays on laws | Medium | High | Executive decree for critical provisions |
